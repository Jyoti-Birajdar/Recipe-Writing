How to make Susla

Introduction

I have seen my mother making Susla(Puffed Rice Upma) several times at home. Susla can be served as a breakfast meal or evening snack. This is a low-fat, healthy snack.

Number of Servings - 3

Preparation time - 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • Puffed Rice (Murmura) - 125 grams
  • Coarsely ground Chana dal- 1/2 cup
  • Peanuts - 2-3 tbsp
  • Green chillies- 2-3
  • Chopped onion - 1 cup
  • Curry leaves - 6-
  • Sugar - 1/2 tbsp
  • Salt - as per
  • Refined oil - 2 tbsp
  • Mustard seeds - 1
  • Cumin seeds - 1
  • Turmeric powder - 1 tbsp
  • Coriander - to garnish

Steps

  1. Rinse the puffed rice(Murmura) in water. To this add coarsely ground chana dal and keep the mixture aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, fry peanuts and remove them in a separate dish.
  3. Now to the oil add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, turmeric powder and curry leaves.
  4. Once mustard seeds and cumin seeds stops sputtering, add chopped green chillies and onion. Fry till the onions become golden brown.
  5. Now add the fried peanuts and soaked puffed rice.
  6. Add sugar and salt as per taste and mix well.
  7. Sprinkle chopped coriander.
  8. Keep a lid on the pan and cook it on low flame about 4-5 minutes.
    9. Delicious Susla is ready to serve!
